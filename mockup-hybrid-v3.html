Senior Environment Artist — Wooga
Environments Mobile Hidden Object
Created richly detailed environments for Wooga's hit hidden-object game. Each scene needed to tell a story while hiding dozens of interactive objects in plain sight. Balancing visual clarity with narrative depth across 20+ scenes and 3 seasonal updates required developing new composition techniques specifically for mobile resolution.

Challenge

Design environments dense enough for hidden-object gameplay while maintaining readability at mobile resolution. Every scene hides 35+ objects without feeling cluttered.

Solution

Developed a layered composition system — foreground, midground, background — with color temperature and directional lighting guiding the player's eye naturally.

Impact

Player engagement exceeded the team average by 15%. The approach was adopted as the studio standard for all new scenes.
